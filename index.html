Understanding Fragrance Notes

Fragrances are composed of different notes that unfold over time. Understanding these notes helps you choose scents that resonate with your personality and preferences.

Top Notes

The initial impression, lasting 5-15 minutes

  • Citrus (Lemon, Bergamot, Orange)
  • Light Fruits (Apple, Berries)
  • Herbs (Lavender, Rosemary)
  • Light Florals (Lily, Neroli)

Heart Notes

The core character, lasting 20-60 minutes

  • Floral (Rose, Jasmine, Lily)
  • Spices (Cinnamon, Nutmeg)
  • Fruity (Peach, Blackcurrant)
  • Green Notes (Vetiver, Grass)

Base Notes

The lasting impression, 2+ hours

  • Woods (Sandalwood, Cedar)
  • Musk & Amber
  • Vanilla & Tonka
  • Leather & Tobacco
